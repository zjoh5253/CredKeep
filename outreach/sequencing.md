# CRE-12 Outreach Sequencing

## Objective
Book initial discovery calls with behavioral-health group practices (3-25 providers) using NCQA 120-day credentialing risk narrative.

## 10-business-day cadence (email + LinkedIn)
1. Day 1: Email #1 (problem-led) + LinkedIn connect with 120-day note.
2. Day 3: LinkedIn follow-up message with one client-relevant question.
3. Day 5: Email #2 with short workflow checklist and CTA for 20-min call.
4. Day 8: Call office line/contact form referencing prior notes.
5. Day 10: Breakup email with clear opt-out and soft CTA.

## Message angle by persona
- Practice owner: lost revenue + delayed onboarding from credentialing lag.
- Operations manager: manual tracking burden and missed renewal windows.
- Clinical director: provider start-date delays hurting access to care.

## Copy skeletons

Would a 20-minute walkthrough next week be useful?

- {{sender_name}}
